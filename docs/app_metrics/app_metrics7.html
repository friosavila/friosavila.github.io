Variance-Covariance Matrix VCV

\[ Var(\beta) = \color{brown}{(X'X)^{-1}} \color{green}{X'} \color{red} \Omega \color{green}{X}\color{brown}{(X'X)^{-1}} \] \[ \Omega = \left( \begin{matrix} \sigma^2_1 & \sigma_{1,2} & \sigma_{1,3} & ... & \sigma_{1,n} \\ \sigma_{1,2} & \sigma_2^2 & \sigma_{2,3} & ... & \sigma_{2,n} \\ \sigma_{1,3} & \sigma_{2,3} & \sigma^2_{3} & ... & \sigma_{3,n} \\ ... & ... & ... & ...& ... \\ \sigma_{1,n} & \sigma_{n,2} & \sigma_{n,3} & ... & \sigma^2_{n} \\ \end{matrix}\right) \]

Homoskedastic Standard errors

\[ \begin{aligned} \sigma_i^2 &=\sigma_j^2 = \sigma^2 \\ \sigma_{ij} &=0 \ \forall \ i\neq j \end{aligned} \]

\[ \Omega = I(N) \sigma^2 = \left( \begin{matrix} \sigma^2 & 0 & 0 & ... & 0 \\ 0 & \sigma^2 & 0 & ... & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \sigma^2 & ... & 0 \\ ... & ... & ... & ...& ... \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & ... & \sigma^2 \\ \end{matrix}\right) \]

Robust Standard errors

\[ \begin{aligned} \sigma_{ij} &=0 \ \forall \ i\neq j \end{aligned} \] \[ \Omega = \left( \begin{matrix} \sigma_1^2 & 0 & 0 & ... & 0 \\ 0 & \sigma_2^2 & 0 & ... & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \sigma_3^2 & ... & 0 \\ ... & ... & ... & ...& ... \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & ... & \sigma_n^2 \\ \end{matrix}\right) \]

1-way Cluster Standard errors

\[ \begin{aligned} g(i)=k : k \in {1,...,G} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \sigma_{i,j} \neq 0 \text{ if } g(i)=g(j) \end{aligned} \]

\[ \Omega = \left( \begin{matrix} \sigma_1^2 & \sigma_{1,2} & 0 & ... & 0 \\ \sigma_{1,2} & \sigma_2^2 & 0 & ... & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \sigma_3^2 & ... & \sigma_{3,n} \\ ... & ... & ... & ... & ... \\ 0 & 0 & \sigma_{3,n} & ... & \sigma_n^2 \\ \end{matrix}\right) = \left( \begin{matrix} \Sigma_1 & 0 \\ 0 & \Sigma_2 \\ \end{matrix}\right) \]

2-way Cluster Standard Errors

\[ \begin{aligned} g_h(i) &=k \ \forall \ h \\ gg_h(i,j)&=1 \text{ if } h: g_h(i)= g_h(j) \end{aligned} \]

\[ \sigma_{ij} \neq 0 \text{ if } gg_1(i,j)=1 \lor gg_2(i,j)=1 \]

\[ \Omega = \left( \begin{matrix} \sigma_1^2 & \sigma_{1,2} & 0 & ... & \sigma_{1,n} \\ \sigma_{1,2} & \sigma_2^2 & \sigma_{2,3} & ... & 0 \\ 0 & \sigma_{2,3} & \sigma_3^2 & ... & \sigma_{3,n} \\ ... & ... & ... & ... & ... \\ \sigma_{1,n} & 0 & \sigma_{3,n} & ... & \sigma_n^2 \\ \end{matrix}\right) \]

How to estimate \(\sigma_{ij}\)?

\[ y_i = x_i \beta + \varepsilon_i \] \[ \hat \sigma_{ij} = \hat\varepsilon_i*\hat\varepsilon_j \]

\[ \begin{aligned} \hat \Omega &= \hat \varepsilon \hat \varepsilon' \circ GG \\ GG_{i,j} &= \max [ gg_1(i,j) , gg_2(i,j) ,..., gg_n(i,j) ] \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} GG &= GG_1 + GG_2 - GG_1 \circ GG_2 \end{aligned} \]

Even Simpler

\[ Var(\beta)_{cc} = Var(\beta)_{1} + Var(\beta)_{2} - Var(\beta)_{1,2} \]

## Homoskedastic Standard errors

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sigma_i^2 &=\sigma_j^2 = \sigma^2 \\
\sigma_{ij} &=0 \ \forall \ i\neq j
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\Omega = I(N) \sigma^2 =
\left(
\begin{matrix}
\sigma^2 & 0 & 0 & ... &  0 \\
0 & \sigma^2 & 0 & ... &  0 \\
0 & 0 & \sigma^2 & ...  & 0 \\
... & ... & ... & ...& ...  \\
0 & 0 & 0 & ...  & \sigma^2 \\
\end{matrix}\right)
$$

## Robust Standard errors

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sigma_{ij} &=0 \ \forall \ i\neq j
\end{aligned}
$$
$$
\Omega =
\left(
\begin{matrix}
\sigma_1^2 & 0 & 0 & ... &  0 \\
0 & \sigma_2^2 & 0 & ... &  0 \\
0 & 0 & \sigma_3^2 & ...  & 0 \\
... & ... & ... & ...& ...  \\
0 & 0 & 0 & ...  & \sigma_n^2 \\
\end{matrix}\right)
$$

## 1-way Cluster Standard errors

$$
\begin{aligned}
g(i)=k : k \in {1,...,G}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\sigma_{i,j} \neq 0 \text{  if  } g(i)=g(j)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\Omega =
\left(
\begin{matrix}
\sigma_1^2     & \sigma_{1,2} & 0         & ...  &  0 \\
\sigma_{1,2} & \sigma_2^2     & 0         & ...  &  0 \\
0            & 0            & \sigma_3^2  & ...  & \sigma_{3,n} \\
...          & ...          & ...       & ...  & ...  \\

\end{matrix}\right) =
\left(
\begin{matrix}
\Sigma_1     & 0            \\

\end{matrix}\right)
$$

## 2-way Cluster Standard Errors

$$
\begin{aligned}
g_h(i) &=k \ \forall \ h \\
gg_h(i,j)&=1 \text{  if  } h: g_h(i)= g_h(j) 
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\sigma_{ij} \neq 0  \text{ if } gg_1(i,j)=1 \lor gg_2(i,j)=1

$$
\Omega =
\left(
\begin{matrix}
\sigma_1^2     & \sigma_{1,2} & 0         & ...  &  \sigma_{1,n} \\

...          & ...            & ...       & ...  & ...  \\
\sigma_{1,n} & 0            & \sigma_{3,n}     & ...  & \sigma_n^2 \\
\end{matrix}\right)
$$
